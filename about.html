About Deep Sea Fishing Mirissa

At Deep Sea Fishing Mirissa, we invite you to experience the untouched beauty of Sri Lanka's southern lagoons in the most peaceful way possible - by boat. Nestled in the heart of Mirissa, our tours are designed for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and anyone who wishes to slow down and connect with the natural world.

Our guided deep sea fishing adventures take you through serene lagoons, lush mangroves, and hidden lagoons, where you can spot vibrant birdlife, playful monkeys, and breathtaking scenery along the way. Whether you're gliding across the calm morning waters during sunrise, or soaking in the golden colors of a sunset paddle, each trip offers a unique perspective of Mirissa's natural charm.

We provide safe, eco-friendly, and memorable experiences led by professional local guides who know the waters inside out. With flexible packages ranging from short scenic trips to half-day explorations, as well as private, family-friendly, and group tours, we ensure there's an adventure for everyone.
